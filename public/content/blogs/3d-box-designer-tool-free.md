## Why Traditional Methods Keep Failing

Here's what the typical packaging ordering process looks like:

### **Method 1: The Email Ping-Pong**

**You:** "I need boxes 30cm x 20cm x 15cm"

**Supplier:** "External or internal dimensions?"

**You:** "Um... internal I think?"

**Supplier:** "What material thickness? Single wall or double wall?"

**You:** "Which one is stronger?"

**Supplier:** "Depends on your product weight. How heavy is it?"

**You:** "About 2 kg... wait, maybe 2.5 kg with packaging materials inside"

**Supplier:** "Do you need handles? Printing? What type of closure?"

**You:** "I don't know. What do other customers do?"

Sound familiar? **This back-and-forth can take days or even weeks.** And at the end, you're still not 100% sure what you're getting.

### **Method 2: Physical Samples**

Some suppliers offer to create a sample box. Sounds perfect, right?

**The Reality:**
- **Cost:** ₹2,000-5,000 per sample
- **Time:** 7-15 days to receive
- **Iterations:** Each change requires a new sample
- **Total investment:** ₹10,000-20,000 and 3-4 weeks

By the time you perfect the design, you've already invested significant money and your launch timeline is compromised.

### **Method 3: The "Trust Me" Approach**

You find a supplier who seems knowledgeable. They say, "Don't worry, we know what you need." You trust them and place an order.

**Success rate?** About 60%.

**The 40% who get it wrong?** They face the Rajesh scenario.

### **The Core Problem:**

All these methods share one critical flaw: **You can't see what you're getting until it's too late.**

You're making a significant financial commitment based on measurements, descriptions, and hope. In 2026, with all our technology, this shouldn't be how businesses operate.

Let's walk through exactly how you'd use this tool to avoid Rajesh's ₹2,30,000 mistake.

### **Step 1: Choose Your Box Type (10 seconds)**

Open the tool and select your box style:
- Regular Slotted Container (RSC) - most common
- Mailer Box - for e-commerce
- Half-Slotted - for display purposes
- Die-Cut Custom - for unique shapes

**Why this matters:** Different box types use different amounts of material and have different structural strengths. Seeing them side-by-side helps you choose the right one for your needs.

### **Step 2: Set Your Dimensions (15 seconds)**

Use the intuitive sliders or type exact measurements:
- **Length:** Your product's longest dimension + cushioning
- **Width:** Second dimension + space for inserts
- **Height:** Depth + any protective layers

**The 3D model updates instantly.** You see exactly how big the box is. No imagination required. No mental math.

**Pro Tip:** Our tool shows both internal and external dimensions. No more confusion.

- **3-Ply (Single Wall):** For lightweight items up to 10 kg

**See the actual material color and texture** on your 3D box. Brown kraft paper, white board, corrugated fluting—it all looks real because we use photorealistic rendering.

- **Choose colors** for text and background elements

**Watch your box transform** from plain brown to branded packaging in real-time.
