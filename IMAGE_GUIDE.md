## 📸 PRODUCT PHOTOGRAPHY TIPS

### General Guidelines for All Product Images:
1. **Consistency:** Use same background color/style for all products
2. **Lighting:** Soft, even lighting - no harsh shadows
3. **Background:** Pure white (#FFFFFF) or light gray (#F5F5F5)
4. **Resolution:** Shoot at 2400x2400px, scale down to 800x800px
5. **Format:** Save as JPG at 85-90% quality
6. **File Size:** Target 100-200KB per image
7. **Color Accuracy:** Ensure boxes look natural brown/kraft color
8. **Sharpness:** Crisp, clear focus on boxes
9. **Composition:** Center products, leave breathing room
10. **Branding:** Subtle, professional - avoid stock photos

### Equipment Recommendations:
- **Camera:** DSLR or high-quality smartphone (12MP+)
- **Tripod:** For stability and consistency
- **Lighting:** Softbox lights or natural window light
- **Background:** White seamless paper or backdrop
- **Editing:** Adobe Lightroom or Photoshop for adjustments

### Post-Processing:
- Adjust brightness/contrast for consistency
- Remove any dust or imperfections
- Ensure white background is pure white
- Sharpen slightly for web display
- Compress without losing quality
